Rock, Paper, Scissors!

The Rules

Classic "Rock, Paper, Scissors" game. Rock smashes scissors. Scissors cut paper. Paper covers rock. You click the button that matches the play you want to choose. The computer picks it's play at the same time. Winner gets a point for the round, first to 5 wins the game. Picking the same as the computer is a tie, "The Cat" wins those rounds. "The Cat" can also win the game if it gets to 5 rounds first.
